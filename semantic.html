How to Brew a Perfect Pour-Over

Aug 1, 2025 • 5 min read

Equipment

  • Gooseneck kettle
  • Filter cone & paper filter
  • Freshly ground coffee

Steps

  1. Rinse filter and preheat cone.
  2. Bloom with 2× coffee weight of water for 30s.
  3. Slow, circular pours until target weight.
